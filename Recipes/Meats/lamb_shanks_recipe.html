Lamb Shanks Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 lamb shanks
  • 2 cups red wine
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Season lamb shanks with salt and pepper.
  2. In a large pot, sear the shanks on all sides until browned.
  3. Add onions, carrots, and celery, cooking until softened.
  4. Pour in red wine, letting it reduce by half.
  5. Add beef broth, garlic, and rosemary. Bring to a simmer.
  6. Cover and braise for 2-3 hours until tender.
  7. Serve shanks with sauce poured over.
