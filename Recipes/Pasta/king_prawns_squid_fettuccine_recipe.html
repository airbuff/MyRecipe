King Prawns & Squid Fettuccine Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g fettuccine pasta
  • 150g king prawns, peeled and deveined
  • 100g squid, sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup white wine
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • Fresh basil, for garnish
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook fettuccine according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and saut√© garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add prawns and squid, cooking until prawns turn pink.
  4. Add cherry tomatoes and white wine, letting it reduce slightly.
  5. Stir in cream and simmer until sauce thickens.
  6. Toss in fettuccine, season with salt and pepper, and garnish with basil.
