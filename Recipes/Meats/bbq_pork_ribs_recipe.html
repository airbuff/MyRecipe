BBQ Pork Ribs Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 rack of pork ribs
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 275°F (135°C).
  2. Season ribs with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder.
  3. Place ribs on a baking sheet, cover with foil, and bake for 2-3 hours.
  4. In a bowl, mix BBQ sauce, vinegar, and brown sugar.
  5. Brush sauce over ribs and broil for 5-10 minutes until caramelized.
  6. Serve with extra sauce on the side.
