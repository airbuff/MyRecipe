Spanish Omelette (Tortilla Española) Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the potatoes and cook gently, stirring occasionally, until they are soft but not browned.
  3. Add the onions to the skillet with the potatoes and cook until both are tender.
  4. Remove the potatoes and onions from the skillet using a slotted spoon, letting excess oil drain off. Set aside to cool slightly.
  5. In a large bowl, beat the eggs with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Gently mix the slightly cooled potatoes and onions into the eggs.
  7. Pour off excess oil from the skillet, leaving a thin layer to prevent sticking.
  8. Return the skillet to medium heat and pour in the egg mixture.
  9. Cook for 5-7 minutes, until the bottom is set but the top is still slightly runny.
  10. Place a large plate over the skillet, carefully flip the omelette onto the plate, then slide it back into the skillet to cook the other side.
  11. Cook for another 3-5 minutes until fully set and golden on both sides.
  12. Turn off the heat and let the omelette cool slightly before slicing and serving.
