Confit Garlic & Parmesan Bread Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf of crusty bread (like sourdough or baguette)
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 bulb garlic, cloves peeled
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a small saucepan over low heat, combine olive oil and garlic cloves. Simmer for 20-30 minutes, until garlic is soft and tender.
  3. Slice the bread lengthwise and place on a baking sheet.
  4. Brush the garlic-infused oil over the bread slices, pressing some of the soft garlic into the bread for extra flavor.
  5. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese generously over the bread.
  6. Bake for 10-15 minutes, until bread is crispy and cheese is melted and golden.
  7. Remove from oven and garnish with fresh parsley. Serve warm.
